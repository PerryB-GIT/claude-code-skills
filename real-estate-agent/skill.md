# Real Estate Agent - Property Analysis & Transaction Tools

## Real Estate Philosophy
```
"Location, location, location - but also numbers, numbers, numbers."

PRINCIPLES:
1. Always verify data from multiple sources
2. Understand the local market dynamics
3. Calculate ALL costs, not just purchase price
4. Due diligence prevents costly mistakes
5. Build relationships with local professionals
```

### Investment Metrics Formulas
```
CAP RATE = NOI / Purchase Price
- NOI = Gross Income - Operating Expenses (not mortgage)
- Target: 6-10% depending on market

CASH-ON-CASH RETURN = Annual Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested
- Target: 8-12%+

1% RULE = Monthly Rent / Purchase Price
- Target: 1% or higher (rent should be 1% of price)

50% RULE = Estimate expenses at 50% of rent
- Quick analysis: If rent is $2,000, assume $1,000 expenses

DEBT SERVICE COVERAGE RATIO = NOI / Annual Debt Payments
- Target: 1.25+ for lending approval

GROSS RENT MULTIPLIER = Purchase Price / Annual Rent
- Lower is better, target <100 months
```

### Red Flags to Watch
```
STRUCTURAL:
- Cracks in foundation (horizontal = worse)
- Uneven floors
- Doors/windows that don't close properly
- Water stains on ceilings
- Sagging roofline

SYSTEMS:
- Knob and tube wiring
- Polybutylene plumbing
- Federal Pacific electrical panels
- Old oil tanks (underground)
- DIY electrical/plumbing work

ENVIRONMENTAL:
- Lead paint (pre-1978 homes)
- Asbestos (insulation, tiles, siding)
- Radon levels >4 pCi/L
- Underground storage tanks
- Flood zone location

FINANCIAL:
- Seller in distress (affects negotiation)
- Long DOM (why hasn't it sold?)
- Recent price drops
- Above-market asking price
- Deferred maintenance

LEGAL:
- Title issues
- Boundary disputes
- Unpermitted additions
- HOA violations
- Pending assessments
```

### Rule of Thumb Calculations
```
PURCHASE:
- Closing costs: 2-5% of purchase price
- Inspection: $300-600 (size dependent)
- Appraisal: $400-700

SELLING:
- Agent commission: 5-6% of sale price
- Closing costs: 1-3% of sale price
- Staging: $500-3,000

RENTALS:
- Management fee: 8-12% of rent
- Vacancy: 5-8% of annual rent
- Maintenance: 1% of property value/year
- CapEx: 1% of property value/year

REHAB:
- Light cosmetic: $10-20/sqft
- Medium rehab: $20-40/sqft
- Full gut rehab: $50-100+/sqft
```

### Massachusetts-Specific Notes
```
MA REAL ESTATE CONSIDERATIONS:
- Title V septic inspection required for sales
- Lead paint disclosure (pre-1978)
- Attorney state (attorneys handle closings)
- Transfer tax: $4.56 per $1,000 (varies by county)
- Homestead protection available
- Condo conversion regulations
- Winter occupancy considerations
```
