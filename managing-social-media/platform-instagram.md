## Content Types Performance

### Reels (Highest Reach)
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Trending audio boosts reach
- Text overlays for sound-off viewing
- Strong CTA at end

**Reel Ideas**:
- Quick tips (3-5 points)
- Before/after transformations
- Day in the life
- How-to tutorials
- Trend participation
- Behind the scenes
- Product showcases
- Customer testimonials

### Carousels (Highest Saves)
- 5-10 slides optimal
- Hook on first slide
- Value on middle slides
- CTA on last slide
- Consistent design template

**Carousel Ideas**:
- Step-by-step guides
- Tips lists
- Myth vs. fact
- Before/after
- Product features
- Educational series
- Industry insights

### Single Images
- High quality (1080x1080 or 1080x1350)
- Strong visual hook
- Brand-consistent editing
- Meaningful caption

### Stories (Engagement)
- Post 3-7 per day
- Mix: polls, questions, quizzes
- Behind the scenes content
- Share feed posts
- User-generated content
- Time-sensitive offers

## Algorithm Optimization

### What Instagram Prioritizes
1. **Relationship** - Content from accounts you interact with
2. **Interest** - Content similar to what you engage with
3. **Timeliness** - Recent content
4. **Frequency** - How often you open app
5. **Following** - How many accounts you follow
6. **Usage** - How long you spend on app

### Boost Your Reach
- Post when audience is active
- Encourage saves and shares (high value signals)
- Reply to comments within first hour
- Use all features (Reels, Stories, Lives, Guides)
- Engage with others before and after posting
- Write long captions (dwell time)
- Ask questions to drive comments

## Hashtag Deep Dive

### Research Process
1. Search your main topic hashtag
2. Look at "Related" hashtags
3. Check competitor hashtags
4. Use Display Purposes for suggestions
5. Analyze top posts in each hashtag

### Hashtag Placement
**Option 1**: In caption (appears in search)
**Option 2**: First comment (cleaner look)
Both work equally well for reach.

## Analytics to Track

### Key Metrics
- **Reach**: Unique accounts that saw content
- **Impressions**: Total views
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes+Comments+Saves+Shares)/Reach
- **Save Rate**: High saves = valuable content
- **Share Rate**: High shares = shareable content
- **Profile Visits**: Interest in your brand
- **Website Clicks**: Conversion intent
- **Follower Growth**: Net new followers

### Healthy Benchmarks
- Engagement Rate: 1-3% (good), 3-6% (great), 6%+ (excellent)
- Save Rate: 2-5% of reach
- Reach: 20-40% of followers per post

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Inconsistent posting
2. Ignoring comments and DMs
3. Only promotional content
4. Poor quality visuals
5. Ignoring Reels
6. Not using Stories
7. Buying followers
8. Using banned hashtags
9. Not engaging with others
10. Giving up too soon (give it 6+ months)
