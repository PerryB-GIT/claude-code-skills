## Content Strategy

### What Works on Facebook

**High Performers**:
- Native video (especially Live)
- Questions that spark discussion
- Local/community content
- Behind-the-scenes
- User-generated content
- Timely/news content
- Contests and giveaways

**Lower Performers**:
- External links (algorithm deprioritizes)
- Overly promotional posts
- Low-quality images
- Text-only posts without hook
- Engagement bait ("Like if you agree!")

### Content Mix

**Ideal Distribution**:
- 40% Video content (Reels, Lives, native video)
- 25% Engaging posts (questions, polls, discussions)
- 20% Visual content (photos, graphics)
- 15% Links and promotions

### Post Formats

**Video (Best reach)**
- Native upload always
- 1-3 minutes optimal
- Captions required (85% watch muted)
- Square or vertical format
- Hook in first 3 seconds

**Facebook Reels**
- Vertical video (9:16)
- 15-90 seconds
- Trending audio helps
- Similar strategy to Instagram Reels

**Live Video (Highest engagement)**
- Notifies followers
- Algorithm boost during and after
- Q&A, behind scenes, events
- 10-60 minutes optimal

**Photos**
- High quality, bright
- 1200x630px (landscape) or 1080x1080 (square)
- Faces perform well
- Minimal text overlay

**Carousels**
- Multiple images in one post
- Great for showcasing products
- Tell a story across slides

**Text Posts**
- Questions drive comments
- Keep under 80 characters for best engagement
- Use line breaks
- Add emoji sparingly

**Links**
- Put link in comments (not post) for better reach
- Or use link posts when traffic is priority
- Custom thumbnails when possible

## Algorithm Understanding

### How Facebook Ranks Content

**Primary Signals**:
1. Who posted (relationship strength)
2. Content type (what you engage with)
3. Engagement level (especially early)
4. Recency (newer content preferred)

**Engagement Priority**:
1. Comments (especially long ones)
2. Shares
3. Reactions (Love > Haha > Wow > Like)
4. Clicks

### Boost Organic Reach

- Post when audience is active
- Encourage meaningful comments
- Reply to every comment quickly
- Go Live regularly
- Use Facebook Stories
- Post native video
- Create discussion-worthy content
- Avoid engagement bait phrases
- Don't edit posts after publishing

- Direct community access
- Algorithm favors Group content

- Gather feedback and insights

1. Clear purpose/niche
2. Set rules and guidelines
3. Seed with initial content
4. Invite existing customers
5. Promote from Page
6. Consistent moderation

**Group Content Ideas**:
- Weekly discussion threads
- Member spotlights
- Exclusive tips/content
- Q&A sessions
- Polls and feedback
- Challenges
- Resource sharing

**Engagement Tactics**:
- Welcome new members personally
- Ask questions daily
- Create themed days
- Recognize active members
- Host live sessions
- Run member-only promotions

## Messenger Strategy

### Response Best Practices
- Respond within 1 hour (business hours)
- Set up auto-responses for after hours
- Use saved replies for common questions
- Personal touch in responses
- Move to resolution quickly

- Ask satisfied customers to review
- Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)


## Posting Schedule

### Best Times (General)
- **Weekdays**: 1-4pm
- **Best days**: Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
- **Peak engagement**: 1-3pm
- **Avoid**: Early morning, late night

### Frequency
- **Minimum**: 3x per week
- **Optimal**: 1x per day
- **Maximum**: 2x per day
- **Stories**: Daily

### Weekly Template
```
Monday: Week kickoff, motivation, tip
Tuesday: Educational content, how-to
Wednesday: Behind the scenes, team
Thursday: User content, testimonials
Friday: Fun content, weekend preview
Saturday: Light engagement, community
Sunday: Prep, inspiration
```

## Facebook Insights

### Key Metrics

**Page Metrics**:
- Page likes/follows (growth)
- Reach (total and post)
- Engagement (reactions, comments, shares)
- Page views
- Actions on page

**Post Metrics**:
- Reach
- Engagement rate
- Click-through rate
- Video views and retention
- Shares (most valuable)

**Audience Insights**:
- Demographics
- Location
- Active times
- Interests

### Healthy Benchmarks
- Engagement rate: 1-5% of reach
- Reach: 5-15% of followers per post
- Response rate: 90%+ (for badge)
- Response time: Under 1 hour

## Facebook Ads (When Needed)

### Boosted Posts vs. Ads Manager
- **Boosted Posts**: Quick, simple, good for engagement
- **Ads Manager**: Full targeting, objectives, optimization

### Best Organic Posts to Boost
- Already performing well organically
- Has clear call-to-action
- Relevant to target audience
- Timely or evergreen

### Small Budget Strategy
- Start with $5-10/day
- Target local area
- Focus on awareness or engagement
- Retarget website visitors
- Lookalike audiences from customers

## Common Mistakes

1. **Only posting links** - Native content gets more reach
2. **Ignoring comments** - Reply to everything
3. **Inconsistent posting** - Regular schedule matters
4. **Ignoring Messenger** - Customers expect quick replies
5. **No video content** - Video dominates reach
6. **Forgetting Groups** - Huge engagement opportunity
7. **Not using Stories** - Free extra visibility
8. **Generic content** - Local and personal wins
9. **Ignoring reviews** - Respond to all
10. **Over-promoting** - 80% value, 20% promotion

## Integration with Instagram

### Meta Business Suite
- Manage both platforms in one place
- Cross-post content
- Unified inbox
- Shared scheduling
- Combined analytics

### Cross-Posting Strategy
- Don't auto-post everything (formats differ)
- Customize captions per platform
- Reels work on both
- Stories work on both
- Adjust hashtags (fewer on Facebook)

### What to Cross-Post
- Reels/short video
- Stories
- Major announcements
- Evergreen content

### What to Keep Separate
- Platform-specific features
- Different audience content
- Hashtag-heavy posts
- Platform-specific trends
