## Content Strategy

### What Performs on LinkedIn

**High Performers**:
- Personal stories with business lessons
- Contrarian opinions
- Industry insights
- Vulnerable/authentic moments
- Practical how-to advice
- Career advice
- Data and research

**Lower Performers**:
- Pure self-promotion
- External links (algorithm dislikes)
- Overly salesy content
- Generic motivation quotes
- Content without personality

### Content Formats

**Text Posts** (Best reach)
- 1,200-1,500 characters optimal
- Line breaks for readability
- Hook in first 2-3 lines
- Lists and frameworks work well

**Document Posts/Carousels** (High engagement)
- PDF uploads as carousels
- 8-12 slides optimal
- Educational content
- Step-by-step guides

**Video** (Growing)
- 30-90 seconds optimal
- Native upload (not links)
- Captions required
- Vertical or square format

**Polls** (High engagement)
- Spark discussion
- Industry-relevant questions
- 3-4 options
- 1-2 week duration

**Articles** (SEO value)
- Long-form thought leadership
- Searchable on Google
- Lower immediate reach
- Good for evergreen content

## Algorithm Understanding

### How LinkedIn Distributes

1. **First hour** - Shows to connections
2. **Measures** - Dwell time, reactions, comments
3. **Expands** - To connections of engagers
4. **Continues** - Based on ongoing engagement

### Engagement Signals (Priority)
1. Comments (especially early)
2. Dwell time (reading/watching)
3. Reactions (Love > Celebrate > Like)
4. Shares (reposts)
5. Follows from post

### Boost Your Reach
- Respond to every comment
- Engage with others' content
- Post consistently (Tue-Thu best)
- Avoid external links in post (put in comments)
- Encourage discussions
- Tag relevant people sparingly

## Posting Schedule

### Best Times
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 8-10am
- **Mid-week**: Highest engagement
- **Avoid**: Weekends, Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Frequency
- Minimum: 2-3x per week
- Optimal: 4-5x per week (daily weekdays)
- Don't exceed 1-2 posts per day

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine (20-30 min)
- 10 min before posting: Engage with others
- Post your content
- 10 min after: Respond to all comments
- Evening: Final engagement round

### Commenting Strategy
- Add value, not just "Great post!"
- Share your perspective
- Ask follow-up questions
- Comment on target accounts
- First 1-2 hours matter most

### Building Relationships
- Connect with comment, not just click
- Engage consistently with same people
- DM to deepen relationships
- Offer help before asking

## Analytics

### Key Metrics
- **Impressions**: Total views
- **Engagement rate**: (Reactions+Comments)/Impressions
- **Profile views**: Interest generated
- **Search appearances**: SEO visibility
- **Connection requests**: Network growth

### Healthy Benchmarks
- Engagement rate: 2-5% (personal), 1-2% (company)
- Comments per post: 10+ (good), 50+ (great)
