# New York Small Business Grant Programs

## State Economic Development Agencies

### Empire State Development (ESD)
**Website:** esd.ny.gov
**Focus:** Economic development, business attraction, incentives

| Program | Description | Amount |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Excelsior Jobs Program | Performance-based tax credits | Varies by jobs/investment |
| Regional Council Capital Fund | Capital grants for job creation | $60M annually statewide |
| FAST NY | Shovel-ready site development | Up to $400M program |
| Global NY Fund | Export promotion grants/loans | Varies |
| Supplier Innovative Finance | Grants for disadvantaged businesses | Up to $5,000 |

**Excelsior Jobs Program Details:**
- Fully refundable tax credits
- Enhanced credits for cleantech/green projects
- Must meet job creation and investment thresholds
- Credits for: job creation, investment, R&D, real property taxes

**Regional Council Capital Fund:**
- $60M allocated in 2025
- For capital-based economic development
- Create/retain jobs, increase economic activity
- Applied through Consolidated Funding Application (CFA)

**FAST NY Program:**
- Up to $400M in site preparation grants
- Track B and Track C actively accepting applications
- Working capital grants up to $500K per site
- Focus on high-tech, cleantech, semiconductor manufacturing

## Demographic-Specific Programs

### MWBE Programs (Nation-Leading)
**Website:** esd.ny.gov/doing-business-ny/mwbe
**Division:** Minority and Women's Business Development (DMWBD)

**2025 Record Achievement:**
- $3.3B disbursed to MWBEs in FY 2024-25
- 31.86% utilization rate (5th consecutive year above 30%)
- Highest statewide MWBE spend in program history
- Discretionary threshold raised to $1.5M

**Certification Benefits:**
- Listed on certified MWBE directory
- Exempt from competitive bidding under $200K
- Priority in contract awards
- Access to lending and bonding programs
- Training and technical assistance
- Business development workshops

**MWBE Initiatives:**
| Initiative | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Sail-Thru Summer | Application assistance |
| Whirlwind Winter | Outreach program |
| Trailblazers in Business | Leadership development |
| Respuesta Rapida | Spanish-language support |
| Second Chance | Re-certification initiative |
| Women Entrepreneurs Rising | Women-focused support |
| Certification Plus | Pilot program for expanded access |

**How to Get MWBE Certified:**
1. Create account on SBS Connect
2. Submit standard or Fast Track application
3. Contact: (212) 803-2414 or mwbe@esd.ny.gov

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Determine Eligibility
- New York-based business
- Industry alignment
- Size requirements
- Regional Economic Development Council area

### Step 2: Get Certified (if applicable)
- **MWBE**: esd.ny.gov/mwbe-certification
- **SDVOB**: ogs.ny.gov/veterans
- **NYC M/WBE**: nyc-business.nyc.gov

### Step 3: Connect with SBDC
- Find your regional center: nysbdc.org
- Free consulting
- Application assistance

### Step 4: Gather Documentation
- NY business registration (Department of State)
- EIN
- Financial statements
- Business plan
- NY tax compliance (Dept. of Taxation & Finance)

### Step 5: Apply
- **Consolidated Funding Application (CFA)**: Most state programs
- **MWBE Portal**: SBS Connect
- **NYSERDA**: nyserda.ny.gov/All-Programs

## Tips for NY Grant Success

1. **MWBE certification is powerful** - NY has nation's highest utilization
2. **Use the CFA** - Consolidated application accesses multiple programs
3. **SBDC network is extensive** - 20 centers + 70 satellites, free help
4. **Clean energy focus** - NYSERDA has substantial funding
5. **Regional councils matter** - Applications go through regional process
6. **START-UP NY** - Tax-free if partnering with university
7. **Excelsior enhanced** - Extra credits for green projects
8. **Global NY for exporters** - Trade show and marketing support
9. **Manufacturing hub** - SBDC has dedicated reshoring support
10. **NYC separate programs** - City has its own M/WBE and incentives
11. **Apply early** - STEP and other programs can exhaust funds quickly
