# Florida Small Business Grant Programs

## State Economic Development Agencies

### Florida Department of Commerce (formerly DEO)
**Website:** floridajobs.org / floridacommerce.com
**Focus:** Economic development, workforce, community planning

| Program | Description | Amount |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Florida Job Growth Grant Fund | Workforce training & infrastructure | Up to $500K |
| State Small Business Credit Initiative (SSBCI) | Capital access for small businesses | $142M total allocation |
| Competitive Florida Partnership | Rural economic development | Varies |
| Community Planning Tech Assistance | Strategic planning grants | Varies |

**SSBCI Details:**
- $142M allocated to Florida
- For businesses up to 750 employees
- Administered by DEO
- Focus on capital access barriers

### Enterprise Florida
**Website:** enterpriseflorida.com
**Focus:** Business attraction, trade promotion, incentives coordination

| Program | Description | Amount |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Qualified Target Industry (QTI) | Tax refunds for job creation | $3K-$6K per job |
| High Impact Performance Incentive (HIPI) | Major project grants | $500K-$1M |
| Quick Response Training (QRT) | Workforce training | Reimbursement |
| Capital Investment Tax Credit (CITC) | Large capital investments | Negotiated |

**QTI Tax Refund Details:**
- $3,000 per net new full-time job
- $6,000 in Enterprise Zone or rural county
- Add $1,000-$2,000 for above-average wages
- Add $2,500 for Brownfield locations
- 20% local match required
- $5M lifetime cap per applicant

**HIPI Requirements:**
- Minimum 50 new jobs + $50M investment (3 years)
- R&D: 25 jobs + $25M investment
- Sectors: clean energy, life sciences, financial services, IT, semiconductors

### Florida Department of Revenue
**Website:** floridarevenue.com
**Focus:** Tax incentive administration

| Program | Description | Benefit |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| R&D Tax Credit | Qualified research expenses | Tax credit |
| Experiential Learning Credit | Apprentices/interns | $2,000 per person (max $10K/year) |
| Sales Tax Exemptions | Manufacturing equipment | Varies |

**R&D Tax Credit:**
- Must be certified QTI business
- 2025 application opens March 2026
- Based on qualified research expenses

### SBDC Programs

| Program | Description | Amount |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| STEP Grant | Export trade show support | Up to $2,500 reimbursement |
| SBIR/STTR Assistance | Federal grant application help | Free consulting |
| Government Contracting | APEX Accelerator services | Free |
| Market Research | Industry analysis | Free |

**STEP Grant Details:**
- Product must be 51%+ US content
- Must be "new to export" or market expansion
- Covers: airfare, lodging, trade show fees, samples
- Maximum $2,000 for sample shipment
- Grants typically $5,000-$10,000

## Disaster Recovery Programs

### Florida Small Business Emergency Bridge Loan
- **Administrator:** Florida DEO
- **Amount:** Up to $50,000
- **Purpose:** Short-term disaster recovery
- **Eligibility:** 2-100 employees, declared emergency impact
- **Terms:** Interest-free, short-term

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Determine Eligibility
- Florida-based business
- Industry alignment
- Size requirements
- Impact area (for some programs)

### Step 2: Get Certified (if applicable)
- OSD Certification: dms.myflorida.com
- Federal SAM: SAM.gov
- VetCert: sba.gov/vetcert

### Step 3: Connect with SBDC
- Find your regional center: floridasbdc.org
- Free consulting
- Grant application assistance
- Market research

### Step 4: Gather Documentation
- Florida business registration (SunBiz.org)
- EIN
- Financial statements
- Business plan
- Florida tax compliance

### Step 5: Apply
- Enterprise Florida programs: enterpriseflorida.com
- DEO programs: floridajobs.org
- Local programs: Contact county/city directly

## Tips for FL Grant Success

1. **Use SBDC network** - 39 centers, free help, grant writing support
2. **STEP grants underutilized** - Great for exporters, easy application
3. **QTI is powerful** - Stack bonuses: rural + wages + brownfield
4. **Get OSD certified** - Opens state contracting doors
5. **Check local FIRST** - County programs often more accessible
6. **Veterans Florida program** - Excellent free training
7. **SBIR/STTR support** - SBDC helps with federal applications
8. **Hurricane recovery** - Emergency bridge loans activate post-disaster
9. **Manufacturing focus** - FloridaMakes provides tech assistance
10. **Tourism angle** - VISIT FLORIDA has co-op marketing programs
