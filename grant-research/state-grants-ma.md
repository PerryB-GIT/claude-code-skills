**Focus:** Real estate, infrastructure, business lending

|---------|-------------|--------|

### MassVentures
**Website:** mass-ventures.com
**Focus:** Tech startups, innovation

| Program | Description | Amount |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| START | Matching SBIR Phase I | Up to $100K |
| Catalyst | Seed funding | $50K-$500K |
| MassVentures Loan | Growth capital | Up to $500K |

**START Program Details:**
- Matches federal SBIR/STTR Phase I awards
- Up to $100K additional funding
- No equity taken
- Straightforward application

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Determine Eligibility
- Business location (MA-based)
- Industry alignment
- Size requirements
- Certification status (MBE, WBE, VBE)

### Step 2: Gather Documentation
- Business registration (Secretary of State)
- Tax ID (EIN)
- Financial statements
- Business plan
- MA tax compliance (Certificate of Good Standing)

### Step 3: Contact Program Staff
- Most MA programs are relationship-driven
- Initial consultation call recommended
- Staff can guide application strategy

### Step 4: Apply
- Online portals vary by program
- Some require pre-qualification
- Follow instructions exactly

## Tips for MA Grant Success

1. **Build relationships first** - MA programs are smaller, more personal
2. **Get certified** - MBE, WBE, VBE certifications open doors
3. **Leverage SBIR** - MassVentures START matches federal awards
4. **Use SBDCs** - Free consulting, application help
5. **Check local first** - City/town programs less competitive
6. **Stack programs** - Combine state + federal + local
7. **Timing matters** - Many programs have annual cycles
